# Password Reset

## How to Reset Your Password
To reset your password, go to bookly.com/login and click "Forgot Password". Enter the email address associated with your account and click Submit. A password reset link will be sent to that email within 5 minutes.

## Reset Link Expiry
Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link has expired, simply request a new one by repeating the process above.

## Email Not Received
If you do not receive a reset email within 5 minutes, check your spam or junk folder. If it is not there, ensure you are using the correct email address. For further help, contact support@bookly.com.

## Locked Accounts
After 10 consecutive failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 30 minutes as a security measure. After 30 minutes, you may try again or reset your password.
