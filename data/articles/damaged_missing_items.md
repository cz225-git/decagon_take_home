# Damaged or Missing Items

## Damaged Items
If your order arrives damaged, contact support@bookly.com within 7 days of delivery. Include your order ID and a photo of the damaged item. Bookly will offer a replacement or full refund at no additional cost, including free return shipping.

## Missing Items
If an item is missing from your order, contact support@bookly.com within 7 days. Include your order ID and a description of what is missing. Bookly will either reship the missing item or issue a refund for it.

## Wrong Item Received
If you received the wrong item, contact support@bookly.com with your order ID and a photo of the item received. Bookly will arrange a free return and send the correct item or issue a full refund.
