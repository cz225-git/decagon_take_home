# Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods
Bookly accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit and debit cards. We also accept PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Bookly Gift Cards.

## Gift Cards
Bookly Gift Cards are available in $10, $25, $50, and $100 denominations. They can be purchased on the website and redeemed at checkout. Gift cards do not expire and cannot be exchanged for cash.

## Payment Security
All payments are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant payment gateways. Bookly does not store full credit card numbers on its servers.

## Billing Issues
If you see an unexpected charge or believe you have been billed incorrectly, contact support@bookly.com with your order ID. Billing disputes are typically resolved within 3–5 business days.
