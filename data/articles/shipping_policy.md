# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days and costs $4.99. Orders over $35 qualify for free standard shipping. Processing time is 1–2 business days before the order ships.

## Express Shipping
Express shipping takes 2–3 business days and costs $12.99. Express orders placed before 2pm ET on a business day are processed the same day.

## Overnight Shipping
Overnight shipping delivers the next business day and costs $24.99. Orders must be placed before 12pm ET. Overnight shipping is not available on weekends or holidays.

## International Shipping
International shipping is available to over 50 countries and takes 10–21 business days. Costs start at $19.99 and vary by destination. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes charged by their country.

## Shipping Delays
Occasionally orders may be delayed due to high volume, weather, or carrier issues. If your order has not arrived within the estimated delivery window, please contact support@bookly.com with your order ID.
