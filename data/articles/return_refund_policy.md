# Return and Refund Policy

## Return Window
Customers have 30 days from the date of delivery to request a return or refund. After 30 days, orders are no longer eligible for return.

## Eligible Items
Items must be in their original condition and packaging to qualify for a return. Books that have been visibly damaged by the customer, written in, or had pages removed are not eligible.

## Non-Eligible Items
Digital books and e-books are non-refundable once purchased and downloaded. Gift cards cannot be returned or refunded.

## Refund Process
Once a return is approved, refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. Customers will receive a confirmation email when the refund has been issued.

## Return Shipping
Bookly provides a free return shipping label for items that arrived damaged or were shipped incorrectly. For all other returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.
