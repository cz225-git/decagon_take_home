# Order Cancellation

## Cancellation Window
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, provided they have not yet entered the processing stage. After 1 hour, cancellation may not be possible.

## How to Cancel
To cancel an order, contact support@bookly.com as soon as possible with your order ID. Include "CANCEL" in the subject line. Our team will confirm whether cancellation is still possible.

## Cancellation Refunds
If your cancellation is successful, a full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

## After Cancellation Window
If your order has already been processed or shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You may instead initiate a return once the order is delivered.
